Refund Policy

We want you to love your GlowExpert purchase. If something isn't right, here's how our refund process works.

Eligibility

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of receiving your order. The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition you received it. Items that have been worn, washed, cut, or altered in any way are not eligible for a refund.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items cannot be refunded:

  • Custom or made-to-order wigs and hairpieces
  • Gift cards
  • Sale or clearance items (unless faulty)
  • Products that have been used, altered, or are missing original packaging

How to Request a Refund

To start a refund, please contact our support team with the following details:

  • Your order reference number
  • The reason for the refund request
  • Photos of the item (if applicable)

You can reach us via the contact details in the site footer or through WhatsApp.

Processing & Timeline

Once your refund request is approved, please ship the item back to us. After we receive and inspect the returned product, your refund will be processed within 5–10 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you'd like a different product, please request a refund and place a new order.
