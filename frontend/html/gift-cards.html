Give the gift of gorgeous hair. GlowExpert gift cards are the perfect present for any occasion.

How Gift Cards Work

Purchase a GlowExpert gift card and receive a unique code. The recipient can apply the code at checkout to use the gift card balance towards any product in our store. Gift cards can be used in full or partially — any remaining balance stays on the card for future use.

Terms & Conditions

  • Gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash
  • Gift cards do not expire
  • Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced
  • Gift cards can only be redeemed on the GlowExpert online store
